Evaluates whether IMU is pointed in the right place at the right time.

Checks if measured azimuth and elevation are within 5 degrees of the commanded angles, and the measured RTC time is within five seconds of the commanded imaging time.

Parameters
yawis the IMU yaw angle
pitchis the IMU pitch angle
azimuthis the commanded azimuth angle
elevationis the commanded elevation angle
